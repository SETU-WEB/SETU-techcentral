# How to sign in & out of Microsoft Office

1. Open **Word**



2. Select **File** > **Account** (some version **More** > **Account**)



3. Under User Information - If signed in with your **@wit.ie** account, click **"Sign out"**



4. Click **Sign in** and sign in with your **@setu.ie** username and password



5. If asked whether to sign in to all apps or just this app, choose **"Sign in to this app only"**



6. **Under Product Information** – Click **Switch License** and sign in with your SETU username and password.
