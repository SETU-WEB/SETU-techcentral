# Setting up Outlook on Windows 11 using your new @setu.ie account.

1. Close **Outlook** completely if it is open



2. In the **search icon on the taskbar** (magnifying glass), type **Control Panel**, and open it



3. Click **Categories** in the top right corner and choose Large icons



4. Select **Mail (Microsoft Outlook)**



5. Click **Show Profiles**



6. Click **Add**



7. Enter a name such as **SETU Outlook** and click **OK**



8. When prompted, sign in using:



&#x20;     a. **Email address:** your **@setu.ie** email address



&#x20;     b. **Password:** your **@setu.ie** password



9. Complete sign-in and **MFA** (Multi Factor Authentication) if prompted



10. Back in the **Mail** window, select **Always use this profile**



11. Choose the new **SETU Outlook** profile and click **OK**



12. Open **Outlook**
