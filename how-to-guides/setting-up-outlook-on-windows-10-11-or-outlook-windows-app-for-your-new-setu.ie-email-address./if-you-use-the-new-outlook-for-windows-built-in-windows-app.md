# If you use the New Outlook for Windows (built in Windows app)

1. Open **Outlook**



2. Click the **Settings icon** (top right)



3. Click **Accounts**



4. Click **Your accounts**



5. Click **Add account**



6. Enter your **SETU email address**



7. Complete **MFA** (Multi Factor Authentication), if requested.



8. Once your **SETU** account has been added, click **Manage** beside you SETU account



9. Click **Set as primary account**
