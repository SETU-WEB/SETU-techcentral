# Remove & Add your @setu.ie email on your phone.

**Part 1** - Removing your @wit.ie account from Outlook on your phone

1. Open the **Outlook** app on your phone.
2. Tap your **profile icon** in the top left corner. (This may be your initials or photo)
3. Tap the **gear icon** (usually bottom left corner)
4. Under **General**, Tap **Accounts**
5. Tap your **@wit.ie** email address
6. **Scroll** to the **bottom** of the screen at click **REMOVE ACCOUNT** (Delete Account on some phones)
7. **Close** and **re-open** the **Outlook app**

This will remove your **@wit.ie** email account from your phone.

It will **NOT** delete your emails as they are stored in the cloud.



**Part 2** - Add your new @setu.ie account to outlook on your phone

**Only do this after your migration is complete**

1. Open **Outlook** on your phone
2. Tap your **profile icon** in the top left corner
3. Tap the **gear icon**
4. Tap **Accounts** (In General)
5. Tap **Add account**
6. Choose **Add an Email Account**
7. Enter your **@setu.ie** email address
8. Sign in using your **@setu.ie** password
9. Complete **MFA** (Multi Factor Authentication) if prompted

Your email will begin syncing automatically.

**Do not keep both @wit.ie and @setu.ie accounts in Outlook at the same time.**
