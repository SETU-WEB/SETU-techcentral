# Switching OneDrive from @wit.ie to @setu.ie

&#x20;**Step 1: Unlink your old @wit.ie OneDrive**



1. Click the **OneDrive** cloud icon in the **system tray** (bottom right)



2. Select **Settings**



3. Go to the **Account** tab



4. Click **Unlink this PC**



5. **Confirm** when prompted



6. Your local OneDrive folder will remain on your computer.





**Step 2: Sign in with your new @setu.ie account**



1. Open **OneDrive** from the **Start menu**



2. Sign in using your **@setu.ie** email address and password



3. When asked for a folder location:



&#x20;        a. Select the existing **OneDrive** folder on your computer



&#x20;        b. **Approve** the prompt to reuse the folder



4. OneDrive will reconnect and resync your files.
