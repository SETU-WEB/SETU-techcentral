## CRM Dynamics

Dear Colleagues,

The Project Confluence team is delighted to announce that our CRM Dynamics tenant migration from WIT to SETU is successfully complete as of last Monday 9<sup>th</sup> March.

The project team worked closely with our migration partners, Storm Technology, to define scope, assess end user impact, design the implementation plan and execute the migration into the SETU production environment.

This is a major milestone in the overall system integration roadmap for the University and would like to take the opportunity to thank all shareholders involved in making this key ICT project happen over the past few months.

Finally, a special mention to Dave Carthy for leading this initiative out and ensuring a smooth path to live throughout the lifecycle of the project.

If you have any questions in relation to the above message, please contact [ProjectConfluence@setu.ie](mailto:ProjectConfluence@setu.ie)



Thank you again for your continued support.
